Ankit Siva

Hi, I'm Ankit, a software engineer at AWS. I work on privacy, fairness and explainability in algorithms.

Previously

  • Software engineer on the Amazon SageMaker Debugger Team. Worked on future profiler capabilities.
  • Software engineer on the Amazon SageMaker Clarify team. Worked on communicating bias and explainability results to users and operational excellence.
  • Software engineering intern at Amazon Book Clubs. Worked on Book Clubs features.
  • Undergraduate research advised by Rachel Cummings. Worked on breaking implementations of differential privacy and on private synthetic data generation.
  • Research intern at Esri's Applications Prototype Lab. Investigated and implemented differential privacy mechanisms for geospatial data.
  • Research intern at the MELT Lab, University of Minnesota. Designed the syntax, semantics and features of a tensor and linear algebra language extension of a custom port of C.
